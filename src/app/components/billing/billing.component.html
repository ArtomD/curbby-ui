How billing works

Your plan tier dictates how many messages you can send to customers or forward to employees per billing period. Once you have have reached your limit for the month you will no longer be able to send messages and the system will stop fowrading to your your subscribers. You can either wait for the next billing period or upgrade to the next tier.

Don't worry, even if you exhausted your allocated volume we will continue to recieve and record any and all messages sent to you by customers so you should never miss their communications.
